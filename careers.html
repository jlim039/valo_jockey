Valo is hiring full-time drivers. If you’re disciplined, safety-first, and take pride in professionalism, we want to hear from you.

Full-Time Driver (Car Jockey / Chauffeur)

Location: Singapore • Schedule: Mainly evenings / late nights • Type: Full-time

What you’ll do

  • Drive customers home safely using the customer’s vehicle
  • Maintain professional conduct and customer service standards
  • Follow dispatch instructions and service procedures
  • Handle handover of vehicle responsibly and securely

Requirements

  • Valid Singapore driving licence (Class 3/3A)
  • Strong road safety record and confident night driving
  • Professional attitude, punctual, calm under pressure
  • Comfortable communicating via Telegram / mobile
  • Singpass verification may be required

Nice to have

  • Prior chauffeur, taxi, PHV, or delivery driving experience
  • Familiarity with popular nightlife areas and routes

We don’t want “fast drivers.” We want safe, consistent, professional drivers who can be trusted with customer vehicles.

Apply via Email

Please include: Name, phone number, licence class, driving experience, and availability. Attach a resume (if any) and a clear photo of your driving licence.
